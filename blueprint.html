Contractor.MBA

Six sessions covering the business side of contracting — financial statements, cash flow management, bonding, and the operational systems that position firms for larger opportunities. Built specifically for construction firm owners, not generic business owners.

6
Sessions
Wednesdays
W1 Understanding Sequencing and Scheduling ▼

Master the fundamentals of sequencing and scheduling in large-scale projects.

  • Identify common scheduling challenges and proven strategies to overcome them
  • Read and interpret project schedules from prime contractors
  • Develop your own milestone-based timeline
W2 Developing Your Own Work Plan ▼

Empower subcontractors to create comprehensive, independent work plans.

  • Reduce reliance on prime contractors for project direction
  • Build accountability structures within your own team
  • Align your work plan with contract deliverables
W3 Being Proactive and Competent ▼

Cultivate a proactive mindset for addressing project challenges before they escalate.

  • Understand the qualities prime contractors value in dependable subs
  • Develop systems for early problem identification
  • Build a reputation for reliability and follow-through
W4 Documenting and Tracking Work ▼

Understand the critical importance of thorough documentation in construction projects.

  • Learn effective methods for tracking progress and recording changes
  • Create audit-ready documentation habits
  • Protect your business in disputes with accurate records
W5 Anticipating and Mitigating Risks ▼

Identify potential risks on large construction projects before they become costly problems.

  • Develop strategies for effective risk assessment and mitigation
  • Build contingency frameworks into your bids and operations
  • Understand insurance, bonding, and liability protection
W6 Recognizing and Documenting Change & Scope of Work ▼

Understand the importance of recognizing changes and scope adjustments during projects.

  • Learn how to document changes effectively for transparency and accountability
  • Navigate change order negotiations with confidence
  • Protect your margins when scope expands unexpectedly

  • All Business Operations workshops (also free to public)
