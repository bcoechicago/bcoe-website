Committees

Seven dedicated committees driving BCOE's mission through events, advocacy, education, and community investment across Illinois.

At BCOE, our mission is to promote economic growth and advancement in Black communities through education, mentoring, advocacy, and legislation. To achieve this, we have established eight dedicated committees — Events, Membership, Finance, Marketing, Education, Legislative, Scholarship, and Industry Engagement — each playing a vital role in supporting our members and fostering community development. These committees provide platforms for collaboration, resource sharing, and professional growth, ensuring that our collective efforts lead to meaningful and lasting impact within the industry.

2025–2026 Committees

Our Committee Structure

Committee
Events
Chair: Michael-Charles Hilson

Plans and executes BCOE's signature events, including the Annual Gala, Golf Outing, Summer Social, and membership meetings. Oversees logistics, programming, and partnerships to ensure each event strengthens engagement and fosters meaningful connections across the industry.

Committee
Membership
Chair: Stephen Rich

Focuses on recruitment, retention, and engagement of BCOE members. Develops outreach strategies, reviews applications, and enhances member experience through onboarding, feedback, and networking initiatives that build a supportive, connected community.

Committee
Finance
Chair: Zola Stewart

Oversees BCOE's financial health, budget planning, and fiscal accountability. Works alongside the Treasurer to ensure responsible stewardship of organizational resources, reviews financial reports, and supports long-term financial strategy and sustainability.

Committee
Marketing
Chair: Leshem Kates Graham

Manages BCOE's communications, digital presence, and public relations. Crafts messaging, designs campaigns, and produces content to elevate BCOE's visibility, highlight member success stories, and attract new partners, sponsors, and members.

Committee
Education
Chair: Shanda McFadden

Develops workshops, certifications, and professional development programs — including The Blueprint Minority Construction Accelerator — to strengthen members' business and technical capacity. Builds partnerships with educational institutions and industry experts to promote continuous learning and workforce growth.

Committee
Legislative
Chair: Andre Noel

Advocates for fair and equitable access within the construction industry. Monitors and engages with local, state, and federal policymakers to influence legislation, contracting policies, and workforce programs that expand opportunities for Black-owned firms across Illinois.

Committee
Scholarship
Chair: Position Open

Leads BCOE Foundation's efforts to invest in the next generation of construction professionals through fundraising, scholarship selection, and student recognition. Partners with schools, sponsors, and community leaders to create educational and career pathways for deserving students.

New Committee — 2026
Industry Engagement
Chair: Cleo Downs

Created by unanimous board vote to return BCOE to its core mission — securing real construction opportunities for Black contractors. Tracks major developments across Illinois including landmark projects, organizes collective contractor participation, builds direct relationships with owners and developers, and advocates loudly for Black contractor inclusion. Cleo Downs chairs the committee; Shon Harris assists. The committee also leads development of a contractor tiering system — matching Tier 1 established firms, Tier 2 growing companies, and Tier 3 emerging contractors to the right projects, training, and advocacy.
