Who We Are

More Than an Organization — A Movement

BCOE is a premier Black business consortium founded in Chicago and proudly serving professionals across Illinois. We empower businesses through strategic partnerships, innovative solutions, and a commitment to excellence — with a vision to expand our impact nationwide.

"Our mission is to promote economic growth and advancement in Black communities through education, mentoring, advocacy, and legislation — unifying our community across Illinois and providing the resources necessary for success, with eyes on national expansion."
