Our Leadership

BCOE is led by an experienced Executive Director and professional staff, guided by a governing Board of Directors committed to our mission across Illinois.

How BCOE is structured: Like all well-governed nonprofits, BCOE operates with a clear separation between those who run the organization and those who govern it. Our Executive Director and staff lead day-to-day operations, execute our mission, manage programs, and serve our members. Our Board of Directors provides strategic oversight, fiduciary accountability, and organizational governance — they set policy, approve budgets, and ensure BCOE fulfills its mission. Both roles are essential. Neither replaces the other.

Organizational Leadership

Executive Director & Staff

The Executive Director leads BCOE's operations, manages staff and programs, serves as the primary liaison to the Board, and is responsible for executing the organization's strategic vision. The staff team supports day-to-day delivery of BCOE's mission.
